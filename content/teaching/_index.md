Balancing "rigor" and "intuition" is both enjoyable and essential in teaching and learning, particularly in computer science and mathematics. Rigorous engagement with theoretical foundations enables us to think critically, challenge prevailing assumptions, and, most importantly, pursue truth without ambiguity.
